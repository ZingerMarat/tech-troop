My Personal Introduction

About Me

Hello! My name is Marat Zinger, and I'm a passionate learner exploring the world of web development.

I have a strong curiosity for how things work under the hood, especially when it comes to technology.

This curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Software Engineering, where I discovered my enthusiasm for building user-centered applications that solve real-world problems. I enjoy combining logic with creativity, and that’s what makes programming so exciting for me.

My Interests

Outside of development, I’m a person with diverse interests that keep me inspired. I enjoy coding not just as a job, but as away to express ideas and build tools that people find useful. In my free time, I love diving into science fiction novels, going for long nature walks, and even experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. These activities help me stay balanced and bring a fresh perspective to the problems I work on. I'm also passionate about sustainable technology and how we, as developers, can contribute to a more efficient and eco-friendly future.

My Goals

My biggest goal is to become a full-stack developer capable of creating powerful, responsive, and accessible web applications. I’m especially interested in working on projects that have a positive impact—be it in education, mental health, or environmental sustainability. In the short term, I aim to strengthen my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, expand my portfolio with meaningful projects, and actively contribute to open-source communities. Long-term, I hope to work in an innovative team or even start my own venture that focuses on creating tools to improve lives through technology.
